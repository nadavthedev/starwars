Star Wars Episode I

Chapter 4 - A New Hope

This episode of the franchise is actually the first one that was released
back in 1977. In the timeline this movie happens 19 years after the events
of Revenge of the Sith. This episode happens during the Empire days.
In this episode Luke Skywalker finds out his dad was a Jedi Knight.
He meets an old man called Obi-Wan Kenobi. Together they meet Han Solo
and Chewbacca and the four join the rebellion to save a princess and destroy
a planet destroyer space station
